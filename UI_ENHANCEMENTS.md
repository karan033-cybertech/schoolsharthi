## Design System

### Colors
- **Primary**: Blue gradient (primary-600 to primary-800)
- **Secondary**: Purple gradient (purple-500 to pink-500)
- **Success**: Green gradient (green-500 to emerald-500)
- **Accent**: Orange/Red gradient (orange-500 to red-500)

### Spacing
- Consistent padding (p-6, p-8)
- Better margins (mb-6, mb-8)
- Card spacing (gap-6, gap-8)

### Shadows
- Light: shadow-md
- Medium: shadow-lg
- Heavy: shadow-xl, shadow-2xl

### Borders
- Rounded: rounded-xl, rounded-2xl
- Border colors: border-gray-100, border-primary-300
